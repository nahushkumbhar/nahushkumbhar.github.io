Thesis, Projects and Internships

MSc Thesis: Improving the Sustainability of Large Infrastructure
using Passive Cooling Techniques

Researched on phase change materials that could store 5 KW of energy. Developed a passive heat sink design that utilized PCM to reduce the load on a Heat Pump cooling system. Designed the system to save up to 47 KWh and 14 kg CO2 per day. Analyzed energy consumption, carbon footprint, and energy savings. Conducted 2D simulation and study of heat exchange for laminar flow through a pipe on ANSYS Fluent.

UG Thesis: Designing Production System for Oil Well

Calculated design properties and pump characteristics for an ESP production system for a vertical oil well. Used MS Excel for data filtration, Pump head, and flow calculations. Calculated the multiphase flow rates of the well fluids using 'PROSPER', a simulation software.

Assessed Project in Reservoir Engineering

Evaluated the oil reservoir properties and analyzed well performance data using 'ECRIN' software. Conducted Pressure Transient Analysis to determine oil reservoir properties. Calculated skin factor and permeability using production history match.

Project Intern: Oil and Natural Gas Corporation, Mumbai

Researched natural gas hydrate formation in gas pipelines. Engineered solutions to mitigate hydrate formation in a high-pressure gas pipeline using chemical methods. Simulated gas hydrate formation using simulation software 'PIPESIM'.

Oil Field Trainee: Hindustan Oil Exploration Company, India

On-field training in oil production operations, equipment (pumps, heat exchangers), and control systems at an oil production facility. Analyzed and compiled a report on oil well blockages and studied chemical-aided oil freeze blockage mitigation. Observed oil field metering and monitoring of export flows of oil and gas.

Chemical Engineering Trainee: Supreme Petrochem Limited, India

In-Plant training in HSE, fire safety, chemical process operations at a continuous production plant. On-field experience in heat transfer, pumping, large-capacity storage, effluent treatment operations. Compiled a detailed technical report on the manufacturing process of polystyrene defining various operations involved.
